Privacy Statement: SCCS is committed to protecting students' privacy. If you wish to remove or alter your information, you now have additional options which are outlined here.
Advanced searching: You can do a more specific search by using keywords in the search box. An advanced search might look like, "first:matt dorm:mertz", which would return everyone named Matt who lives in Mertz. The accepted keywords are:
  • first
  • last
  • year
  • email
  • dorm

Important:The dorm keywords are as follows:
  • To search for Alice Paul use AP
  • To search for Mary Lyon use ML
  • To search for David Kemp use DK
  • To search for Strath Haven use Strath
All other dorms are searchable by their normal full name.
